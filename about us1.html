Hole in One Golf Resorts, founded in 2010 in Lisbon, Portugal, is a leading company in the management and development of luxury golf resorts, led by Alexandr Knight. The company offers world-class golf courses, premium hospitality services, and organizes international tournaments, in addition to developing upscale residential communities and providing professional training services. With a strong focus on innovative design and environmental sustainability, the company aims to be the top destination for golf enthusiasts, creating exceptional golfing experiences in breathtaking natural settings.
